MISSION

At ZCOER NSS Cloth Donation Initiative, our focus is on relieving the burden of clothing insecurity. Through the collection of gently used and new clothing donations, we aim to support individuals and families experiencing economic hardships and homelessness. By promoting donation and reuse, we actively work towards reducing textile waste and lessening our environmental impact. Join us in our efforts to make a positive difference in people's lives while caring for our planet.

VISION

We envision a world where clothing insecurity is eradicated, and every individual has access to clean, comfortable clothing. This vision drives our efforts and serves as a beacon, inspiring us to create a lasting impact on communities worldwide. Together, we strive to turn this vision into reality and foster positive change for those in need.
